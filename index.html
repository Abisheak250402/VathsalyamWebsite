About Us

VATHSALYAM is a special project of Sevabharathi, dedicated to empowering differently abled children. We strive to create a nurturing environment where every child can learn, grow, and thrive to their fullest potential.

Our Vision

To build an inclusive society that offers equal opportunities and celebrates diversity.

Our Mission

To provide quality education, training, and rehabilitation for differently abled children, fostering independence and dignity.

Our Services

Therapies

Physiotherapy, Speech Therapy, and Occupational Therapy to aid physical and cognitive development.

Special Education

Customized learning programs for children based on their individual abilities and requirements.

Vocational Training

Skill development programs to encourage independence and prepare children for future opportunities.

Events & Programs

Each and every function such as Yoga, Independence Day, Onam, Krishna Jayanti, Diwali, Children's Day, Differently Abled Day, Pongal, and more are celebrated with Sevabharathi's responsible persons, sponsors, parents, and the community at Vathsalyam School. All celebrations are filled with joy and unity.
