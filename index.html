About Us

Vathsalyam, School is a special project of Sevabharathi, which was started on 17/08/2010 at Padmanapaburam Sevabharathi office, only with 2 students. But more than 70 students have benefitted from this.

It serves different types of activities to get progress in rural and urban areas with a lot of cultural, economical and social changes in the society.

And now our "VATHSALYAM" is located in Krishnancoil Street, Keralapuram, Kanyakumari District with 35 students, 4 Special Educators, Principal, Co-ordinator, along with 3 helpers.

Our Services
