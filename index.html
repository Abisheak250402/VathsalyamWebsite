About Us

Vathsalyam School is a special project of Sevabharathi, which was started on 17/08/2010 at Padmanapaburam Sevabharathi office, only with 2 students. But more than 70 students have been benefitted. It serves different types of activities to bring progress in rural and urban areas with cultural, economical, and social changes in society. Now, our "VATHSALYAM" is located in Krishnancoil Street, Keralapuram, Kanyakumari District with 35 students, 4 Special Educators, Principal, Co-ordinator, along with 3 helpers.

Special Education

Tailored learning programs for children with intellectual challenges.

Life skills training to encourage independence and confidence.

Inclusive events and programs to build community integration.
