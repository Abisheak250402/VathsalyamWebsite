About VATHSALYAM

Vathsalyam School is a special project of Sevabharathi, launched on 17 Aug 2010 at Padmanabhapuram Sevabharathi office with two students. More than 70 students have benefited from our programs. We work in both rural & urban areas to bring cultural, economic and social change through education, therapy and community programs.

Today VATHSALYAM is located at Krishnancoil Street, Keralapuram, Kanyakumari District, serving 35 students with 4 Special Educators, a Principal, Coordinator and 3 support staff.

Vision & Mission
Empowering each child — Community focused

Vision

Develop the potential of each Individual and stretch it into the maximum.

Mission

Together with the community to create social changes for the differently abled — make it work.

Our Services
Therapies • Education • Community

TRAINING for AUTISM STUDENTS

We provide training for Autism students & some students after necessary rehabilitation and training have gone back to mainstream schools.

VOCATIONAL TRAINING

Vocational Training Centre are designed to enable a differently abled person to secure and retain suitable employment.

YOGA

Children with special needs have various physical and mental disabilities which affect their mental attitude.

OCCUPATIONAL THERAPY

Occupational therapy helps students increase functional independence in daily life; periodic assessments are conducted by therapists.

PHYSIO THERAPY

The main aim is to reduce physical deformities and increase mobility. Children are encouraged and parents are happy with the progress.

SPEECH THERAPY

Speech therapy is an important part of intervention for cognitive and social development in children with developmental disabilities.

SPECIAL EDUCATION

Training in movement, hygiene, language, reading, writing, calculations, social communication, hobbies and practical education by experienced special teachers.

MEDICAL CHECKUP

Twice-a-year medical checkups are conducted; medicines are prescribed/supplied and each student is monitored individually.

HEALTHY FOODS

Natural & nutritional meals such as sprouts, nuts, fruits and vegetables are prepared and served to boost students' immunity.
