VATHSALYAM

SPECIAL SCHOOL FOR SPECIAL CHILDRENS

About Us

Vathsalyam is a special project of Sevabharathi, which was started on 17/08/2010 at Padmanapaburam Sevabharathi office with only 2 students. More than 70 students have benefitted from this initiative. Currently, VATHSALYAM is located in Krishnancoil Street, Keralapuram, Kanyakumari District with 35 students, 4 Special Educators, a Principal, a Coordinator, and 3 Helpers.

Vision & Mission

Vision: "Develop the potential of each Individual and stretch it into the maximum."

Mission: "Together with the community to create social changes for the differently abled — make it work."

Our Services

Training for Autism Students

We are providing training for autism students. Some students, after necessary rehabilitation and training, have gone back to mainstream schools.

Vocational Training

Vocational Training Centres are designed to enable differently abled persons to secure and retain suitable employment.

Yoga

Yoga helps children with special needs strengthen both their body and mind, improving their physical and mental health.

Occupational Therapy

Helps students increase their functional independence in daily life. Periodical assessments are conducted by therapists.

Physiotherapy

Reduces physical deformities and increases mobility. Children enjoy physiotherapy and benefit greatly from it.

Speech Therapy

Essential for improving cognitive and social development among children with developmental disabilities.

Special Education

Focuses on body movement, hygiene, communication, reading, writing, calculations, etc., by qualified educators.

Medical Checkup

Medical checkups are conducted twice a year. Monthly medicine is supplied by Saradha Homeo Hospital.

Healthy Foods

Natural & nutritional foods like sprouts, fruits, nuts, and vegetables are prepared and served to improve students' immunity.

Festive Celebration

Functions like Yoga Day, Onam, Krishna Jayanti, Diwali, Children’s Day, Pongal, etc. are celebrated with joy and community participation.

Sports Activities

Various sports like 50m/100m/200m running, long jump, frog race, musical chair are trained by an ex-army official.

Competitions and Prizes

Our students participate in interschool singing, dancing, and speech competitions and bring pride to our school with their talents.

Outdoor Activities

Students are taken to public and tourist places every month to develop social knowledge and outdoor experience.

Special Efforts

Slow learners are taught the normal school syllabus and sent to mainstream schools. 6 students have transitioned successfully.

Extra Curricular Activities

Activities like Bharatanatyam, singing, drawing, drama, reading, writing, social communication and more are trained by professionals.

What Can We Do?

Events such as weddings, anniversaries, and birthdays can be shared with the children at our school.
