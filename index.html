VATHSALYAM

SPECIAL SCHOOL FOR SPECIAL CHILDRENS

About Us

Vathsalyam is a special project of Sevabharathi, started on 17/08/2010 at Padmanabhapuram Sevabharathi office with only 2 students. More than 70 students have benefitted since. The school supports cultural, economic, and social progress. Now located in Krishnancoil Street, Keralapuram, Kanyakumari District, it has 35 students, 4 special educators, a principal, a coordinator, and 3 helpers.
