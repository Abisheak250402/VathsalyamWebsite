About VATHSALYAM

VATHSALYAM School is a special project of Sevabharathi, launched on 17 August 2010 at Padmanabhapuram Sevabharathi office with just two students. Since then, more than 70 students have benefited from our programs.

Our school engages in diverse activities that promote progress in both rural and urban areas, fostering cultural, economic, and social change within the community.

Today, VATHSALYAM is located at Krishnancoil Street, Keralapuram, Kanyakumari District, serving 35 students with the dedicated guidance of 4 Special Educators, a Principal, a Coordinator, and 3 support staff.
