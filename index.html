About Us

Vathsalyam School is a special project of Sevabharathi, which was started on 17/08/2010 at Padmanapaburam Sevabharathi office with only 2 students. More than 70 students have benefitted from this initiative. It serves different types of activities to bring progress in rural and urban areas, bringing cultural, economical, and social changes in society.

Now, our "VATHSALYAM" is located at Krishnancoil Street, Keralapuram, Kanyakumari District with 35 students, 4 Special Educators, Principal, Co-ordinator, and 3 helpers.

Vision

Develop the potential of each Individual and stretch it to the maximum.

Mission

Together with the community to create social changes for the differently abled and make it work.
