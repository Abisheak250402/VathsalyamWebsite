VATHSALYAM

SPECIAL SCHOOL FOR SPECIAL CHILDRENS

About Us

Vathsalyam, School is a special project of Sevabharathi, which was started on 17/08/2010 at Padmanapaburam Sevabharathi office , only with 2 students. But more than 70 students were benefitted with this. It serves different types of activities to get a progress in rural and urban areas with a lot of cultural, economical and social changes in the society. And now our "VATHSALYAM" is located in Krishnancoil street, Keralapuram, Kanyakumari District with 35 students, 4 Special Educators, Principal, Coordinator, along with 3 helpers.

Vision

"Develop the potential of each Individual and stretch it into the maximum"

Mission

"Together with the community to create social changes for the differently abled — make it work"

Training for Autism Students

We are providing training for Autism students & some students after necessary rehabilitation and training have gone back to mainstream schools.

Vocational Training

Vocational Training Centres are designed to enable differently abled persons to secure and retain suitable employment.

Yoga

Children with special needs have various physical and mental disabilities which affect their mental attitude. Yoga helps strengthen both body and mind.

Occupational Therapy

Helps students increase their functional independence in daily life. Periodical assessments are conducted by occupational therapists.

Physiotherapy

Reduces physical deformities and increases mobility. Children enjoy physiotherapy and parents are happy to send their children to school.

Speech Therapy

Important for children with developmental disabilities to improve cognitive and social development.

Special Education

Provides body movement training, hygiene, communication, reading, writing, calculations, and more by experienced special educators.

Medical Checkup

Twice a year, medical checkups are held. Monthly medicines are provided by Saradha Homeo Hospital. Students are assessed individually.

Healthy Foods

Natural and nutritional foods like sprouts, nuts, fruits, and vegetables are served to improve immunity and health.

Festive Celebration

Yoga, Independence Day, Onam, Krishna Jayanti, Diwali, Children’s Day, Differently Abled Day, Pongal, and Republic Day are celebrated with Sevabharathi officials, sponsors, parents, and community members.

Sports Activities

Various sports including running, musical chair, jumping, frog race, etc. are trained by an ex-army official.

Competitions and Prizes

Students participate in competitions like singing, dancing, and speech at other schools and win prizes, bringing pride to our school.

Outdoor Activities

Students are taken to public and tourist places monthly to enhance social knowledge and outdoor experience.

Special Efforts

Slow learners are trained in the normal syllabus and transitioned to regular schools. Six students have successfully moved on this way.

Extra Curricular Activities

Includes training in Bharatanatyam, singing, drawing, drama, reading, writing, hygiene, hobbies, and more by qualified teachers through the special education system.

What Can We Do?

Events such as weddings, anniversaries, and birthdays can be shared with our children.
