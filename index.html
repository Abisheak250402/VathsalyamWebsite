About VATHSALYAM

Vathsalyam School is a special project of Sevabharathi, started on 17/08/2010 at Padmanapaburam Sevabharathi office with only 2 students. More than 70 students have benefited from it. The school serves different activities to promote progress in rural and urban areas with cultural, economic, and social changes. Currently, VATHSALYAM is located in Krishnancoil Street, Keralapuram, Kanyakumari District, with 35 students, 4 Special Educators, a Principal, a Co-ordinator, and 3 helpers.
