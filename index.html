About Us

Vathsalyam School is a special project of Sevabharathi, which was started on 17/08/2010 at Padmanapaburam Sevabharathi office, only with 2 students. More than 70 students have benefited from this. It serves different types of activities to improve rural and urban areas with cultural, economical, and social changes in society.

Currently, our "VATHSALYAM" is located in Krishnancoil Street, Keralapuram, Kanyakumari District with 35 students, 4 Special Educators, a Principal, Coordinator, along with 3 helpers.

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To empower differently abled children with education, training, and cultural development so they can lead fulfilling lives in society.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality education, therapies, and skill development programs tailored to the unique needs of each student.

Our Services

Training for Autism Students

We are providing training for Autism students & some students after necessary rehabilitation and training have gone back to mainstream schools.

Vocational Training

Vocational Training Centre are designed to enable a differently abled person to secure and retain suitable employment.

Yoga

Children with special needs have various physical and mental disabilities which affect their mental attitude.

Occupational Therapy

Helps students increase functional independence in daily life; assessments are conducted by occupational therapists.

Physiotherapy

Reduces physical deformities and increases mobility, making children more active and confident.

Speech Therapy

Supports cognitive and social development in children with developmental disabilities.

Special Education

Training for body movement, hygiene, language, reading, writing, social skills, and more by qualified teachers.

Medical Checkup

Bi-annual checkups with monthly medicine supply; each student is assessed for abilities and development.

Healthy Foods

Nutritious foods like sprouts, nuts, fruits, and vegetables are provided to boost immunity and health.
