VATHSALYAM

SPECIAL SCHOOL FOR SPECIAL CHILDRENS

About Us

Vathsalyam is a special project of Sevabharathi, started on 17/08/2010 at the Padmanabhapuram Sevabharathi office with only 2 students. More than 70 students have benefitted since. The school supports cultural, economic, and social progress. Now located in Krishnancoil Street, Keralapuram, Kanyakumari District, it has 35 students, 4 special educators, a principal, a coordinator, and 3 helpers.

Vision

"Develop the potential of each individual and stretch it into the maximum."

Mission

"Together with the community to create social changes for the differently abled — make it work."

Autism Training

Rehabilitation for autism students. Some return to mainstream schools.

Vocational Training

Skill-based training for employability of differently abled persons.

Yoga

Improves physical and mental well-being for better condition management.

Occupational Therapy

Enhances daily activity independence. Periodically conducted by experts.

Physiotherapy

Improves movement and reduces physical deformities.

Speech Therapy

Supports communication and cognitive development in special children.

Special Education

Includes body movement, hygiene, social skills, reading and writing.

Medical Checkup

Biannual health checkups with medicines from Saradha Homeo Hospital.

Healthy Foods

Nutritious foods like sprouts, fruits, and nuts to improve immunity.

Festive Celebration

All major festivals celebrated with sponsors and local community.

Sports Activities

Events like frog race, musical chair, jumps, and races are conducted.

Outdoor Activities

Monthly trips to develop social knowledge and life exposure.

Special Efforts

Slow learners trained and transitioned into mainstream education.

Extra Curricular

Includes Bharatanatyam, drama, speech, drawing, and Thirukkural.

What Can We Do?

Events like wedding anniversaries, birthdays, and parent anniversaries can be celebrated with our children.
