VATHSALYAM

SPECIAL SCHOOL FOR SPECIAL CHILDRENS

About Us

Vathsalyam is a special project of Sevabharathi, which was started on 17/08/2010 at Padmanapaburam Sevabharathi office with only 2 students. Over time, more than 70 students have benefited from this initiative. It supports a variety of activities to bring about cultural, economic, and social progress in rural and urban areas. Today, VATHSALYAM is located in Krishnancoil Street, Keralapuram, Kanyakumari District, and serves 35 students with the help of 4 special educators, a principal, a coordinator, and 3 helpers.

Vision

"Develop the potential of each individual and stretch it into the maximum."

Mission

"Together with the community to create social changes for the differently abled — make it work."

Training for Autism Students

Training is provided for autism students. After proper rehabilitation, some are reintegrated into mainstream schools.

Vocational Training

Helps differently abled persons to secure and retain suitable employment through skill development.

Yoga

Yoga strengthens both body and mind, helping students with special needs manage their conditions better.

Occupational Therapy

Improves functional independence in everyday activities. Conducted periodically by professionals.

Physiotherapy

Improves mobility and reduces physical deformities. Fun for kids and appreciated by parents.

Speech Therapy

Essential for cognitive and language development in children with developmental disabilities.

Special Education

Includes training in body movement, hygiene, communication, and academics by expert educators.

Medical Checkup

Biannual medical checkups and monthly medicine supplies provided by Saradha Homeo Hospital.

Healthy Foods

Nutritious food like sprouts, nuts, fruits, and vegetables are served to improve health and immunity.

Festive Celebration

All major festivals are celebrated with Sevabharathi members, sponsors, parents, and the local community.

Sports Activities

Includes 50m, 100m, 200m runs, long jump, high jump, frog race, musical chair and more — trained by an ex-army officer.

Competitions and Prizes

Students win prizes in singing, dancing, and speech events at other schools and colleges.

Outdoor Activities

Monthly trips to public and tourist spots help improve social knowledge and awareness.

Special Efforts

Slow learners are trained with the normal syllabus and sent to regular schools after their improvement.

Extra Curricular Activities

Training includes dance, drama, GK, drawing, Bharatanatyam, reading, writing, and life skills.

What Can We Do?

Events like wedding anniversaries, birthdays, and parent anniversaries can be celebrated with our children.
